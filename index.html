Roadmap Of Hackathon


1

Registration Process

Students can apply in a team of 2 to 5 members and individully also. Those who'll apply individually , they'll get a chance to team up with other individual.


Eligibility:-
  • # Laptop
  • # Internet Connectivity
  • # Student should be from 2nd or 3rd year
2

Presentation Round

All registered candidates will be called with their laptops, and they will be given 2 hours time to think their idea, design their presentation and present in front of experts.

Date- 2 November, 2022

Timing:-
# 10:00 to 12:00 PM (Presentation Designing)
# 12:00 to 2:00 PM (Presenting)

3

Team Qualification

# Based on presentation round, Top 10 teams will be selected based on their presentation.
# They will advance to finale round. That is project making day.

4

Project Building Round

# Top 10 Teams will advance to finale round.
# Timing- 9:30 AM to 4:00 PM.
# Projects will be collected and submitted at devfolio website.
# Students will explain their projects in front of experts between 4:00 PM to 5:00 PM.

Date- 3 November, 2022

5

Closing ceremony

Based on Project and project presentation, everyone will be
graded and result will be declared within 3-5 working days.
